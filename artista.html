BIOGRAPHY

Who is M.I.A.?

Her personal experience makes Borders more authentic.

  • Real name: Mathangi Arulpragasam.
  • British artist with Tamil Sri Lankan roots.
  • Known for mixing pop music, visual art and social criticism.
PERSONAL HISTORY

Connection with migration

She lived through war, displacement and asylum.

  • She grew up between Sri Lanka and the UK during civil conflict.
  • Her family had to flee and search for safety.
  • That is why Borders speaks about something she knows closely.
ARTISTIC STYLE

Music with a message

M.I.A. uses visibility to talk about war, power and inequality.

  • She mixes hip-hop, electronic music and global influences.
  • Her songs often create political debate.
  • Her visual style reinforces the message of her lyrics.
BORDERS

Why she wrote the song

Borders came out in 2015, at the peak of the refugee crisis.

  • More than 1 million people crossed the Mediterranean that year.
  • M.I.A. wanted to highlight the human cost of borders.
  • The song turns the news into a moral question.

Art can say what politics avoids.

That is the clearest way to understand M.I.A.'s role in Borders.
